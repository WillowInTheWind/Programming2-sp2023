BlackJack Rules and Instructions

How to Win

The goal of Blackjack is to get the sum of your cards to equal 21, if neither players cards are 21, its the person with the highest sum. however if you ever go over 21 you bust and the other player wins

How to play

You start with 2 cards with some sum. On your turn you can either hit or pass. When you hit you are given a new card and when you pass you get no cards. This cycle continues until you either pass or bust and go over 21. The CPU does the same, assuming neither players have busted you compare values and see who wins.
